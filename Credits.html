First and foremost, I’d like to extend my deepest gratitude to John Hansen and his family for sharing the transcription of their grandfather’s diary and permitting his remarkable story to be told. Their generous assistance throughout the development of this project is immensely valued. An enormous amount of appreciation is held for New Zealand epidemiologist Dr. Jennifer Summers, who, in the midst of the stresses associated with the COVID-19 pandemic, took the time to reply to an inquiry from a student on the other side of the world. Dr. Summers and her colleagues’ previous research laid the groundwork, but her ongoing help made this project a reality. Special thanks to Sam Underwood, whose talents as a developer are only surpassed by his enthusiastic support, no matter the challenge. I would also like to thank the members of my Master’s Committee for their guidance and support.
