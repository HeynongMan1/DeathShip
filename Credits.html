I’d like to extend my deepest gratitude to John Hansen and his family for sharing the transcription of their grandfather’s diary and permitting his story to be told. Their generous assistance throughout the development of this project was immensely valued. An enormous amount of appreciation is held for the kindness of Dr. Jennifer Summers and Lynne Blake, who, in the midst of the stresses associated with the COVID-19 pandemic, took the time to reply to an inquiry from a student on the other side of the world, and made this project possible. Special thanks to Sam Underwood, whose talents as a developer are only surpassed by his enthusiastic support, no matter the challenge. I would also like to thank the members of my Master’s Committee for their guidance throughout this endeavor. Committee Chair Julie Elman’s support predates the scope of this project and began in 2013 when she first interviewed me as a prospective freshman at the School of Visual Communication. Finally, I would like to thank my parents for their wisdom and unwavering support at every step of this voyage.
